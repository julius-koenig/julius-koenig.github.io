# Privacy Policy
## Information Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

No user accounts are created, no analytics or tracking services are used, and no personal data is shared with the developer or any third parties.

## Local Data Storage

Any app settings, preferences, or game progress are stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to the developer.

## Apple Services

This app may use Apple services such as Game Center and In-App Purchases.

These services are provided and operated by Apple. Any personal information required to provide these services is processed by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not receive or have access to this personal information.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
