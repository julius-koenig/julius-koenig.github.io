# Support

Thank you for using Powerup 2048!

If you have questions, encounter a bug, or would like to suggest a feature, I'd be happy to hear from you.

## Contact

Email: koenig_julius@proton.me

Please include the following information if you're reporting an issue:

- Device model (e.g. iPhone 17 Pro)
- iOS version
- App version
- A brief description of the problem
- Steps to reproduce the issue (if possible)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is my game progress stored?

Your progress is stored locally on your device.

### Does the app collect personal data?

No. The app does not collect or store personal information. For more details, please see the Privacy Policy.

### Response Time

I aim to respond to support requests within a few business days.
